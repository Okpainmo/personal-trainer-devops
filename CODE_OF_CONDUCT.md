# Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, direct, and focused on improving the
Personal Trainer DevOps documentation.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear and constructive language.
- Give technical feedback with enough context for the author to act on it.
- Respect different levels of familiarity with the project.
- Keep discussions on topic and tied to the work being reviewed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information, credentials, or internal access details.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Any behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or unproductive.

## Reporting

Report concerns to the repository maintainers or project leads through the approved team channel.
Maintainers are expected to review reports promptly and handle them with appropriate discretion.
